What is service design today?

As design is changing, the aim of this project is to take a picture of those changes and help clarifying how service design is influencing the whole field.

This publication is the result of a research conducted by  Matteo Remondini  over the last few months in London, where service design as a practice was born and is steadily evolving.

Feel free to navigate the website or  download the full report 

Home

The senior professionals interviewed for this research worked for relevant firms like IDEO, Livework, Fjord, Engine and Facebook among the others - or as freelance consultants.
They have been selected according to their different backgrounds and perspectives - service designers, PSSD graduates, senior UX designers and also the founder of a service design recruiting company took part in this project- so that the final picture is quite rich.

This website presents both the full transcript of those interviews and the selection of the most relevant results, divided per topic.
To learn more about this project, have a look to the  About  section.
